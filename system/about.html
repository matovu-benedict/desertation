Description of UMU DMS

The University Dissertation Information System (UDMS) is meant to address most of the challenges that face students during the process of developing university dissertations. It will be used in supervision, monitoring, implementation of dissertations and submission to the university library where it is stored. The system will also act as an archive for all previous dissertations, thus acting as a point of reference for next generation students. In case of an appeal by the student, the system will provide evidence based on previous interaction between the student and supervisor.

The UMU Desertation Management Ssystem aims at :

1.Enabling supervisors and students to communicate during the research period.
2.Enabling supervisors to track all the assigned students for supervision.
3.Enabling all students to be supervised equally during this pandemic period since all movements were restricted.

Stake Holders

Below are some of the stakeholders of the system .

Student

A regestered student from the university who is to be supervised

Supervisor

This is a qualified and registered lecturer from the supervisor

Research-Cordinator

Cordinator assigns supervisors to students and monitors the system

Librarian

Monitors the research process on behalf of the university and clears students for graduation.
